Introduction

This website is a platform which gives information about people who are willing to buy similar product / service in a particular city / region. The information is given about those who are registered with this platform and are willing to share their details.

This information is used by prospective buyers to contact each other and jointly look for seller / dealer who can give the best deal for their cumulative demand.

This information is also useful for prospective sellers of product / service, since they can get demand in bulk for their product / services. For bulk demand, they will not mind giving further discount.

Thus this platform creates a win - win situation for both, the buyer and the seller.
